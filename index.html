I am currently flinishing my PhD in the Thermal fluids and Thermal Processing lab under the guidance of Dr Milind. A. Jog, Dr Urmila Ghia and Dr Leonid A. Turkevich. The project I work on is from NIOSH and is to investigate dustiness of a material. I conduct high fidelity CFD simulations to understand the aerosolization of particles in high Reynolds number flow (Re20000). This is a Multiphysics multiphase problem where the boundary conditions move with time, adding complexity to the physics involved. I also do the data processing of the aerosolized lagrangian dust particles. This helped me reveal structures and clustering in the dust cloud. I also do work on vortex shedding from bluff bodies, vortex shedding suppression by modifying the surface of the bluff body.I am very intrigued and like to solve rich problems and I am looking for new opportunities to expand the horizon of my research.
