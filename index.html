About Me

I am currently finishing my PhD in the Thermal Fluids and Thermal Processing Lab under the guidance of Dr. Milind A. Jog, Dr. Urmila Ghia, and Dr. Leonid A. Turkevich. My research focuses on high-fidelity CFD simulations to understand the aerosolization process in high Reynolds number flows (Re = 20,000). This involves multiphase, multiphysics modeling where moving boundary conditions add complexity.

I also investigate vortex shedding and suppression mechanisms from bluff bodies by modifying surface characteristics and introducing porous coatings. My interests lie in exploring rich fluid dynamics problems and advancing computational modeling techniques.
